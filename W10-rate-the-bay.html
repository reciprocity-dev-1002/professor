Quick Reference: RATER

Five dimensions customers use to judge service quality:

R
Reliability — Did they do what they said they'd do, correctly and on time?
A
Assurance — Did the employees seem knowledgeable, competent, and trustworthy?
T
Tangibles — Were the facilities, equipment, and employees professional-looking?
E
Empathy — Did the staff treat you like a person, not a transaction?
R
Responsiveness — Were they willing and quick to help when something came up?

Zone of Tolerance

Every customer has a range of acceptable service — not perfect, but good enough. That's the Zone of Tolerance.

Below
Frustrated — unacceptable
Within
Acceptable — met expectations
Above
Delighted — exceeds expectations
