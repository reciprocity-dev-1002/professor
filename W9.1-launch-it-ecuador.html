The Scenario
Your team is launching a solar lantern for rural Andean communities in Ecuador. Target customers earn $200–$400/month, have no reliable electricity grid, and currently pay for kerosene. Cloud cover is heavy from October through February. You have 8 points to spend across 5 product decisions. Every point is a tradeoff.

For each decision: (1) make your own pick and explain your reasoning, (2) discuss with your group, then (3) lock in a group answer together. Your role shapes what you should prioritize.
