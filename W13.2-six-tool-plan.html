Week 13 · Exercise 2 · The Brief
The Netflix Brief
Read this together. You'll build one IMC plan — one brand voice, 2 or 3 tools, within $3.5M.

The show

Stranger Things: Hawkins Class of '86 — a spin-off following a new group of Hawkins High students one year after the events of the final season. 8 episodes. All drop on a Friday in October 2026.

The target

Fans of the original series (16–35) who are at real risk of not coming back for a spin-off they didn't ask for.

The Action

Open Netflix and watch episode 1. That's it. If they don't watch the pilot, none of this mattered.

AIDA — how a fan gets to "press play"
Attention
They notice the show exists
Interest
They lean in, learn more
Desire
They want to watch
Action
They open Netflix, press play
These stages describe one consumer's journey. Your tools run in parallel — the ranking shows where each does its heaviest lifting, not the only stage it touches.

The rules

One brand voice across 2 or 3 tools. Full $3.5M is yours to spend.
Any of the six tools can be picked. Two people in a group can write for the same tool — in Phase 2 you'll decide whose tactic is stronger.

Step 1 (~8 min): Pick one tool, write your own tactic.

Step 2 (~12 min): As a group, pick 2 or 3 tools within $3.5M, write the group's headlines, Big Idea, Handoff, and Risk.

Step 3 (~3 min): Score your plan on brand consistency.
